subroutine MISCLN ( )

SUBPROGRAM: MISCLN POSTS MISCELLANEOUS FIELDS PRGRMMR: TREADON ORG: W/NP2 DATE: 92-12-20.

ABSTRACT: THIS ROUTINE HAS BECOME THE CATCH-ALL FOR MISCELLANEOUS OUTPUT FIELDS POSTED BY THE ETA POST PROCESSOR. CURRENTLY THIS ROUTINE POSTS THE FOLLOWING FIELDS: (1) TROPOPAUSE LEVEL Z,P, T, U, V, AND VERTICAL WIND SHEAR, (2) MAX WIND LEVEL Z, P, U, AND V, (3) FD LEVEL T, Q, U, AND V, (4) FREEZING LEVEL Z AND RH, (5) CONSTANT MASS (BOUNDARY) FIELDS, (6) LFM LOOK-ALIKE FIELDS, AND (7) NGM LOOK-ALIKE FIELDS.
