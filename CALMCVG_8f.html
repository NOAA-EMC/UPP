SUBPROGRAM: CALMCVG COMPUTES MOISTURE CONVERGENCE PRGRMMR: TREADON ORG: W/NP2 DATE: 93-01-22

ABSTRACT: GIVEN SPECIFIC HUMIDITY, Q, AND THE U-V WIND COMPONENTS THIS ROUTINE EVALUATES THE VECTOR OPERATION, DEL DOT (Q*VEC) WHERE, DEL IS THE VECTOR GRADIENT OPERATOR, DOT IS THE STANDARD DOT PRODUCT OPERATOR, AND VEC IS THE VECTOR WIND. MINUS ONE TIMES THE RESULTING SCALAR FIELD IS THE MOISTURE CONVERGENCE WHICH IS RETURNED BY THIS ROUTINE.

USAGE: CALL CALMCVG(Q1D,U1D,V1D,QCNVG) INPUT ARGUMENT LIST: Q1D - SPECIFIC HUMIDITY AT P-POINTS (KG/KG) U1D - U WIND COMPONENT (M/S) AT P-POINTS V1D - V WIND COMPONENT (M/S) AT P-POINTS

OUTPUT ARGUMENT LIST: QCNVG - MOISTURE CONVERGENCE (1/S) AT P-POINTS
